The visual path

Learn AI agents from scratch.

No framework required. Every concept starts at first principles and gets a visualization you can actually play with. Free, honest about costs and failure modes, and updated as the field moves.

Why from scratch? Because it's what the best builders recommend — Anthropic's own guidance is to understand the underlying loop before reaching for frameworks. Most courses skip that step. This path is that step.
